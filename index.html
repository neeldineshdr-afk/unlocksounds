About the Studio

Unlock the Sounds is a premium home-based phonics studio in Ghodasar–Maninagar, Ahmedabad, led by Mrs. Neelam (M.Com, B.Ed). We specialise in structured phonics instruction for children aged 4–8 years, helping them decode words confidently and read with clarity.

Our approach focuses on systematic sound learning, guided blending practice, pronunciation improvement, and steady confidence building in a calm and nurturing environment.

Program Structure

Foundation Level

Letter sounds, phonemic awareness, and early decoding skills.

Blending & Reading

CVC words, sentence reading, and guided fluency development.

Advanced Phonics

Digraphs, long vowels, paragraph reading, and pronunciation refinement.

Classes are conducted thrice a week in mini batches of 5–6 students to ensure individual attention. Personal one-to-one batches are also available.
